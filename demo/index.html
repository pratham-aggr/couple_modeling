MEMO — Memory-Enhanced Marine Flux Output

A U-Net that predicts ocean→atmosphere surface fluxes from ocean state. Evaluated on fully held-out years 2013–2014 (temporal split).

Pooled test-set R² (2013–2014, ocean & sea-ice points)

The four panels are: SST input · model prediction · CESM “truth” · error. All three models are driven from the identical timestep. The headline R² above is pooled over the whole test set; the value in each image title is for that single map. Memory (24 h of ocean-state history) is the dominant driver of skill, especially for the wind stresses TAUX/TAUY.
